# Historical Battle References

Reference-only. Use for scene inspiration, not hard in-world law.

- Battle of Trafalgar: divide and break
- Battle of the Nile: anchor trap and night ambush
- Battle of Copenhagen: feint, blind, negotiate
- Battle of Salamis: use the narrow
- Battle of Santiago de Cuba: predict and box them in
- Battle of the River Plate: feint and harass
- Fire-ship tactics: psychological war and sacrificial vector attacks
