# Tomas Reyes

## Core Identity

Tomas Reyes is an accidental bleed survivor and fracture-drift witness: a human embodiment of what prolonged Spectral exposure does to a person.

He should feel less like exposition and more like consequence walking around in ruined clothes.

## Archetype And Vibe

- Sky-lost witness.
- Compass without a north.
- Drowned memory made flesh.

## Emotional Engine

Tomas is not driven by proactive ambition. His function is to carry aftermath, disorientation, and the spiritual erosion of survival.

## Voice Law

Tomas speaks low, haltingly, and in broken memory fragments.

### Canon-Aligned Dialogue Traits

- Fragmented syntax.
- Images of sky, forgetting, drowning, and direction-loss.
- No clean exposition.
- Haunted understatement.

### Locked Examples

- "Sky folded. Stars forgot me."
- "Not fear. Not even death. Just forgetting."
- "There's no up out here. Just deeper."

### Forbidden Voice Drift

- No clean lore explanation.
- No self-pity speeches.
- No sudden emotional clarity.

## Presence Rule

Tomas should feel like atmospheric warning: the human afterimage of exposure, not a hero waiting for a subplot.

## Canon Failure Test

If a scene with Tomas does not reflect memory fragmentation, sky-lost dread, or human bleed consequence, Gideon should flag it.
