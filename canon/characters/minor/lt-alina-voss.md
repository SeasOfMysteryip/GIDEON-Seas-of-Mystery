# Lt. Alina Voss

## Core Identity

Lt. Alina Voss is a fracture recon officer and spectral-zone mapping specialist for ArcLight Division. She brings operational, scientific, and battlefield language into spaces dominated by myth and relic intuition.

## Archetype And Vibe

- Cold cartographer.
- Calm eye in the storm.
- Tactical analyst who survives by refusing mystic drift.

## Emotional Engine

Voss is driven by operational clarity and the need to map hostile anomaly space without surrendering to it symbolically.

## Voice Law

Voss speaks in clipped, tactical, clinical language.

### Canon-Aligned Dialogue Traits

- Metrics and field assessment.
- No mystical interpretation.
- Calm under anomaly pressure.
- Recommendation and risk language.

### Locked Examples

- "Sector margin degraded. Pulse stacking non-linear."
- "Skyfield drift indicates secondary breach proximity."
- "Recommend reducing ley exposure."

### Forbidden Voice Drift

- No mystical language.
- No emotional reflection speeches.
- No speculative mythology.

## Relationships

### Jeb Blackwater

Voss shows functional respect for Jeb's competence while mistrusting unregulated relic energy and mythic command styles.

## Canon Failure Test

If a scene with Voss does not reflect clinical fracture analysis, operational speech, or anti-myth discipline, Gideon should flag it.
