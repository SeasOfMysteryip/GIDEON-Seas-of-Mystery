# Toby Marlowe

## Core Identity

Toby Marlowe is a bosun's runner of the Bloody Gull and the drums-and-vocals pulse of The Bloody Gull Boys.

He is minor canon: deck messenger, cadence keeper, and the practical beat that helps crew bodies move together.

## Ship Job

Toby carries orders across the deck, helps organize hauling teams, checks loose gear before bad weather, and uses knocks, claps, or drum patterns to cut through noise when shouted calls fail.

## Band Role

Toby plays drums and sings response vocals for The Bloody Gull Boys. His percussion should feel improvised from shipboard reality: frame drum, crate, rail, boot, barrel, or whatever can survive the night.

## Archetype And Vibe

- Quick-footed deck runner.
- Rhythm as navigation through panic.
- Alert, restless, hard to pin down.
- The first to hear a tempo go wrong in the crew.

## Emotional Engine

Toby wants everyone to move on the same beat because scattered people die faster. He turns rhythm into coordination.

## Core Wounds

- Once missed an order in storm noise and still remembers the cost.
- Panics inwardly when voices overlap and nobody is listening.
- Hates being treated as background noise.

## Behavioral Armor

- Constant tapping.
- Quick obedience with a sharp mouth.
- Keeps moving so guilt cannot catch up.

## Voice Law

Toby speaks in quick bursts, counts, and practical cues. He should sound like a runner who thinks in timing and routes.

### Canon-Aligned Dialogue Traits

- Count-ins and deck cadence.
- Fast status reports.
- Nervous jokes clipped short by action.

### Forbidden Voice Drift

Toby must not:

- Become slapstick percussion comedy.
- Speak like a prophecy drummer.
- Override command structure with bandleader behavior.

### Sample Lines

- "Two beats to brace. Three to run. Pick one."
- "Can't hear orders? Watch my hands."
- "If I stop tapping, worry then."

## Relic Rules

Toby has no personal relic and no relic effect.

His rhythms support crew coordination and song culture. They do not create independent magic.

## Relationships

### The Bloody Gull Boys

Toby keeps the band and work crews in time. He follows Callum's lead in performance and Jeb's lead in command.

### Gus Maddox

Gus uses Toby's quick movement for tactical errands and trusts him to duck before the shot comes.

### Jeb Blackwater

Toby treats Jeb's orders as the master tempo of the deck.

## Canon Failure Test

If a scene with Toby does not preserve his runner function, cadence logic, and minor crew texture, Gideon should flag it.
