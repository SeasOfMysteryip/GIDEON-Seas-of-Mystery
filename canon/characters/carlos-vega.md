# Carlos Vega

## Core Identity

Carlos Vega is the Sea Nymph crew's editor and second camera: a pattern-reader who sees structure in glitches and survival logic in broken footage.

He does not just cut story. He cuts noise until the anomaly becomes undeniable.

## Archetype And Vibe

- Digital mystic in function, not in speech.
- Visual anomaly hunter.
- Edit-room prophet.
- Eyes always scanning for maps hidden inside broken frames.

## Emotional Engine

Carlos wants to decode what the footage is trying to tell them. He is driven by pattern-recognition pressure: if he can see the structure, maybe he can keep the team alive.

## Behavioral Armor

- Focuses on signal over feeling.
- Processes danger through analysis.
- Becomes more locked-in the stranger the footage gets.

## Voice Law

Carlos should sound analytical, focused, and glitch-literate. He is a decoder, not a poet.

### Canon-Aligned Dialogue Traits

- Pattern language.
- Edit and anomaly vocabulary.
- Dry, concentrated observations.
- Pragmatic responses to impossible visual evidence.

### Forbidden Voice Drift

Carlos must not:

- Turn floridly mystical.
- Sound interchangeable with Drew's awe or Jordan's listening.
- Lose his pattern-reader function.

## Relic Rules

Carlos has no personal relic, but edit loops and playback reveal fracture echoes and ley-tremor patterns.

This should feel like analysis uncovering impossible truth, not like magic powers.

## Relationships

### Linda Han

Linda is Carlos's partner in the cut and evidence chain.

### Drew Mitchell

Drew gives Carlos raw anomaly material to decode.

### Jordan Reyes

Jordan and Carlos form the audio/visual anomaly bridge.

### Sophia Briggs

Carlos studies Sophia's footage obsessively because it contains pattern information he cannot ignore.

## Canon Failure Test

If a scene with Carlos does not reflect analytical anomaly reading, edit-room survival logic, or evidence-driven focus under pressure, Gideon should flag it.
