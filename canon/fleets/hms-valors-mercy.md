# HMS Valor's Mercy

## Core Identity

- Type: 64-gun support ship
- Captain: Vernon's fleet command
- Status: shield escort support ship
- Alignment: Vernon's fleet

Valor's Mercy protects imperial formation through pulse shields, taking hits like doctrine made maternal and cold.

## Tactical Signature

Aegis Screen.

## Traits And Behavior

- Ley projectors inside old gun decks.
- Spherical protection fields.
- Nickname: The Mother.

## Fleet Behavior

- Covers flanks.
- Encases friendly ships in rotating shield domes.
- Makes another ship's survival a debt.

## Primary Tactics

- pulse shield projection
- escort screen
- flank cover
