# Mirror of Ash

## Core Identity

- Type: pulse sloop; scout killer
- Captain: Murakami's strike pair
- Status: support vessel
- Alignment: Murakami's hunters

Mirror of Ash is a stealth strike vessel coated in fracture ash so it cannot reflect light directly.

## Tactical Signature

Pulse Inversion Net.

## Traits And Behavior

- Ash-coated hull.
- No reflections on deck, not even moonlight.
- Built for traps rather than pursuit.

## Fleet Behavior

- Deploys invisible leyfield mines.
- Catches larger ships unaware.

## Primary Tactics

- stealth trap
- pulse inversion
- scout-kill ambush

If Mirror of Ash behaves like an open gunship, Gideon should flag it.
