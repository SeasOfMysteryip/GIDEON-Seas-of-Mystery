# Queen Anne's Revenge

## Core Identity

- Type: spectral dreadnought
- Captain: Edward "Blackbeard" Thatch
- Status: hell-burned leviathan; apex siege platform
- Alignment: Blackbeard's domain

Queen Anne's Revenge is a ruin given warform. She does not sail water so much as force it aside. Her arrival changes the emotional weather of a battle before her cannons speak.

## Tactical Signature

Suppression and domination. Slow, inevitable, dread-forward.

## Traits And Behavior

- Burned black from wars against time and fracture.
- Ley fractures trail behind her like a comet tail.
- Bones, oaths, and conquered memory are sealed into her hull mythology.
- Her presence alone can destabilize weaker crews.

## Fleet Behavior

- Acts as fleet anchor and gravity well.
- Uses relic-infused broadsides, dread projection, and overwhelming position.
- Should feel calm even when destroying things.

## Primary Tactics

- Shatterwake Coil
- suppression broadside
- relic-disruptive dread pressure

## Supporting Ships

- The Devil's Lantern: decoy and flanker that mimics Queen Anne's Revenge signatures.
- Widow's Wake: silent drift-and-hit executioner.
- Cutter of the Deep: targeted relic extraction cutter.

## Gideon Failure Test

If Queen Anne's Revenge feels fast, scrappy, or merely loud, Gideon should flag it.
