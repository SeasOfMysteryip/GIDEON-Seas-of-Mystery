# HMS Thunderwake

## Core Identity

- Type: bomb ketch; disruption ship
- Captain: Vernon's fleet command
- Status: relic destabilizer
- Alignment: Vernon's fleet

Thunderwake fires scrambler rounds that turn active relic weapons against their own timing.

## Tactical Signature

Charge Disruption Barrage.

## Traits And Behavior

- Crystal warheads.
- Bell rings before cannons fire.
- Built to detonate leyfields on impact.

## Fleet Behavior

- Disables active relic weapons.
- Seals rear vectors in Bladewall formations.

## Primary Tactics

- scrambler barrage
- relic destabilization
- rear seal
