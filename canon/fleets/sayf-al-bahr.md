# Sayf al-Bahr

## Core Identity

- Type: fracture hunter-killer
- Captain: Darak al-Mhar
- Status: predator and trader of death
- Alignment: Darak's fleet

Sayf al-Bahr does not sail seas; she hunts them. She is fast, opportunistic, and visible most clearly through the damage her bite has already made.

## Tactical Signature

Chaos as precision.

## Traits And Behavior

- Bow curves like a scimitar.
- Skips across dimensional seams.
- Rivets bleed ley heat.
- Cannons fire spectral leyflare rounds.

## Fleet Behavior

- Prefers shadowed stalking over open assault.
- Outpaces the Gull in straight runs but struggles in tight ley pockets.

## Primary Tactics

- False Flag Spiral
- Echo Bleed Maneuver
- Fracture Lure

## Supporting Ships

- Maiden of Dust: reverse-flow pulse runner.
- Lantern Thief: relic signal forger.
- Coil of the Dead: trap-drift mine layer.

## Gideon Failure Test

If Sayf al-Bahr fights honorably, directly, or without transaction logic, Gideon should flag it.
