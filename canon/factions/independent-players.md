# Independent Players

Free agents, relic scavengers, brokers, mercenaries, and wildcards motivated by profit, survival, revenge, or myth access.

## Goals

- crack mythic truth for personal gain
- sell relic paths
- survive between bigger powers

## Economic Function

Independent players are one of the main engines of the bleed economy.

They turn myth pressure into:

- salvage
- rumor trade
- route-brokerage
- relic fencing
- freelance anomaly labor
- survival services in haunted zones

## Typical Members

- black market relic traders
- descendants unaware of blood ties
- former Protectors turned mercenaries
- rogue documentary mirrors
