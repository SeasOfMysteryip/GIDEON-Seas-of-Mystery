# Power Flow

## Active Player Temperatures

- Blackbeard: quiet gravity and long-game immortality
- Ching Shih: abyssal sovereignty and command finality
- Murakami: current control and strategic inevitability
- Charles Vane: burn-it-all fury and direct ruin
- Vernon Harrow: dead-imperial law and command obsession
- Darak al-Mhar: opportunistic survival and clever tide-gambling

## Underworld Flow

- Blackbeard is the underworld kingpin and primary gravity well.
- Ching Shih is not subordinate; she is an equal storm-side power.
- Murakami acts as tactical arm and ally of convenience.
- Vane is tolerated chaos until extinguished.
- Vernon and Darak operate outside the formal chain.

## Bleed-Economy Rule

Power in this world is not only measured in relic ownership or fleet strength.

It is also measured in control over:

- routes
- rumor
- salvage
- surveillance
- anomaly labor
- access to haunted geographies
