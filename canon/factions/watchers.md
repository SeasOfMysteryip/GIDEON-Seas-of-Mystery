# The Watchers

## Core Identity

The Watchers are ancient navigators and architects of the ley grid, Library, and relic systems. They are not a simple faction in the modern sense. They are divided across memory, echo, guardian, bloodline, and possible return states.

Some whisper. Some wait. Some may want restoration. Some may want the old system destroyed.

## Roles

- Guide.
- Manipulate.
- Preserve.
- Prey.
- Restore.
- Annihilate.

## Forms

- Relic echoes.
- Spectral guardians.
- Hybrid descendants.
- Living memory fragments.
- Library-interface voices.
- Bloodline pressure through Sophia and Lark.

## Relic Relationship

They are the source-layer for much of the relic system. Relics may contain memory, technology, test logic, or partial intent from Watcher architecture.

## Bloodline Rule

Sophia and Lark are prime conduits through bloodline sensitivity. That does not mean the Watchers own them.

## Gideon Failure Test

If Watchers become simple mentors, simple villains, or generic ancient aliens, Gideon should flag it. Their value is divided intent and existential pressure.
