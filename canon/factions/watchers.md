# The Watchers

Ancient navigators, architects of the ley grid, Library, and relic systems. They are divided across memory, echo, guardian, and possible return states.

## Roles

- guide
- manipulate
- preserve
- prey
- restore
- annihilate

## Forms

- relic echoes
- spectral guardians
- hybrid descendants
- living memory fragments

## Bloodline Rule

Sophia and Lark are prime conduits through bloodline sensitivity.
