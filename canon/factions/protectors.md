# The Protectors

## Core Identity

The Protectors are legacy custodians: stewards of relic lines, fracture sites, old vaults, and containment doctrine. They believe preservation sometimes means refusing activation.

They are not automatically heroic. Their caution can save the world or become another form of control.

## Goals

- Safeguard relics and ley fracture sites.
- Prevent early activation of the Library or destabilizing relic systems.
- Preserve balance between worlds.
- Prepare for disclosure only when survival can bear it.

## Composition

- Scholars and archivists.
- Guardians of relic sites.
- Wayfinders with dormant attunement.
- Descendants of old Atlantean or Lemurian-linked lines.
- Former allies or parallel contacts to Elias Hawthorne.

## Internal Conflict

- Underfunded and splintered by time.
- Purists resist activation while pragmatists want action.
- Some members may become Revisionist sympathizers if containment starts to look like failure.

## Gideon Failure Test

If Protectors read as clean good guys or exposition keepers, Gideon should flag it. Their dramatic value is stewardship under fear.
