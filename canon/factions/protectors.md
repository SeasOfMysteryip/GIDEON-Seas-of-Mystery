# The Protectors

Legacy custodians descended from Atlantean or Lemurian bloodlines, devoted to containment over activation.

## Goals

- safeguard relics and fracture sites
- prevent early Library activation
- preserve balance between worlds

## Composition

- scholars
- archivists
- guardians
- seekers
- dormant bloodline wayfinders
