# The Revisionists

## Core Identity

Revisionists are power rewriters: militarists, technocrats, cultists, black-ops operators, corporate ghosts, and fracture theorists who believe history can be corrected if the right people seize the machinery.

They are dangerous because their methods can sound rational while their ethics rot underneath.

## Goals

- Weaponize relics and fractures.
- Rewrite or prune history through controlled outcomes.
- Force Watcher return, imitate Watcher power, or convert old systems into usable dominance.
- Keep the public blind while building private control.

## Methods

- Black sites and containment vessels.
- Relic weaponization and signal counterfeit.
- Bloodline targeting.
- Mind, memory, or perception manipulation.
- Private expedition funding and front operations.

## Conflict Axis

- Declared enemies of Protectors when containment blocks power.
- Predators of Independent Players when market access becomes useful.
- Interested in Sophia and Lark as ignition points, not merely people.

## Season Two Guardrail

Current Season Two canon may assign Revisionist pressure through Benjamin Briggs and related infrastructure. Do not import older named leadership as active canon unless the writer restates it.

## Gideon Failure Test

If Revisionists become generic science villains or cultists without the core idea of correcting reality through control, Gideon should flag it.
