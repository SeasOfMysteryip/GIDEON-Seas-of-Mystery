# The Revisionists

Militarists, technocrats, cultists, and black-ops power rewriters who want to weaponize relics and reshape history.

## Goals

- weaponize relics and fractures
- rewrite history through control
- force Watcher return or usable dominance

## Methods

- black sites
- relic weaponization
- mind control
- bloodline targeting
