# Blackbeard's Ring

## Core Identity

Blackbeard's Ring is a sovereign tether relic: command, ambition, ghost-fleet control, and life extended through spectral hunger.

## Function

- commands dead fleets with exact authority
- channels ley surge in storm conditions
- binds drowned souls into Blackbeard's command structure
- tethers the bearer's continued existence to spectral dominion

## Limitation And Cost

- feeds on ambition and ley hunger
- cannot be safely removed after full bond
- punishes hesitation and diminishing will
- acts more like a shackle than a crown

## Bearer Rule

Blackbeard is the canon bearer. The ring should feel inseparable from his command identity and not like an accessory someone can casually pass around.

## Symbiosis

- flares near the Crown of the Abyss
- responds to Compass presence
- intensifies at fracture crossings and fleet convergence

## Faction Pressure

This relic is a dead-command artifact tied to Blackbeard's long game. Others may covet it, but its real law is bonded ambition.

## Episode Status

Active through Season One council, convergence, and manipulation beats, with future imbalance pressure seeded through its clash with the Crown.

## Failure Test

Flag drift if the Ring feels removable, morally neutral, or disconnected from Blackbeard's sovereign will and spectral debt.
