# Lysara's Locket

## Core Identity

Lysara's Locket is a siren-memory relic built on longing, persuasion, and emotional entrapment. It is not an Atlantean circuit piece and should feel different from the main relic system.

## Function

- influences listeners through mesmeric song
- amplifies longing, regret, and emotional vulnerability
- carries fragments of Lysara's memory
- can stabilize or worsen storm and fracture states through song resonance

## Limitation And Cost

- haunts both bearer and listener with lingering melody
- erodes emotional stability through repeated use
- traps users in unresolved desire loops
- is especially dangerous to those already bound to Lysara's memory

## Bearer Rule

Jeb is the original emotional bearer. Darak becomes the active political bearer after the trade for Sophia. Those are different relationships and should read differently.

## Symbiosis

- pulses near Sophia through emotional-frequency cross-resonance
- serves as a counterpoint to war-pulse relics like the Beacon

## Faction Pressure

Political manipulators and council players value it as leverage. It should read as a high-risk emotional weapon, not a battlefield power relic.

## Episode Status

Gifted to Jeb in Episode 102, haunts his arc through mid-season, and is traded to Darak in Episode 108 for Sophia's freedom.

## Failure Test

Flag drift if the locket acts like neutral music magic, loses its Lysara tether, or stops exacting emotional cost from whoever uses it.
