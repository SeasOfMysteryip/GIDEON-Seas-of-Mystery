# Shell of Memory

## Core Identity

The Shell of Memory is a language and revelation relic. It exposes buried memory, signal, and Library-facing resonance through partial, emotionally charged transmission.

## Form And Visual Signature

- Iridescent conch whose surface ripples like oil on water.
- Atlantean glyphs shift with emotion and ley flux.
- It fits in a hand but carries the weight of drowned civilizations.
- Even in silence, it seems to hold distant voices.
- When active, voices rise as drowned hymns, ley static, and layered memory.

## Function

- Translates ley pulses, fracture pressure, and memory-coded systems.
- Opens auditory communion with buried systems, dead memory, and spectral response.
- Unlocks sound-based relic systems tied to the Library.
- Recovers personal and ancestral memory, but only as unstable fragments.

## Limitation And Cost

- Reveals only partial truth.
- Strong emotion activates glyph burn through Sophia's body.
- Ancient thoughts and voices blur identity.
- The Shell syncs to Sophia's heartbeat; without it, she may still hear voices that are not there.
- Each use risks emotional drift away from the crew and toward older loyalties.

## Bearer Rule

Sophia is the primary bloodline user. The Shell should feel intimate and invasive, not like a neutral lore terminal.

## Symbiosis

- Helps decode Compass flow into audible fragments.
- Spyglass of Storms confirms what the Shell transmits.
- Old Red's dice and Tuning Core can spike or stabilize Shell resonance.
- After Old Red's death, the Shell can partially relay the Bloody Gull's ship-consciousness.

## Faction Pressure

- Memory-seeking powers want it as an interpreter of buried systems.
- Murakami and other system-hunters should desire it, but not access it cleanly.
- Ghost fleets react instinctively, as if the Shell reminds the dead of their names.

## Episode Status

Season One active relic central to Sophia's memory burden and Library-facing activation.

## Failure Test

Flag drift if the Shell gives clean exposition, reveals without distortion, or acts without emotional consequence.
