# Tuning Core

## Core Identity

The Tuning Core is the harmony engine of the Gull. Through Old Red it binds ship, crew, chant, and relic pulse into survivable rhythm.

## Function

- stabilizes ley currents under shipboard pressure
- conducts shared rhythm between crew, relics, and vessel
- anchors fracture survival during storms
- transfers into the Gull after Red's death, making the ship itself a living relic

## Limitation And Cost

- mirrors Old Red's state directly
- weakens with heartbreak, crew panic, and bodily decline
- collapses structurally when Red dies
- demands shipwide emotional coherence to hold strongest form

## Bearer Rule

Old Red is the living anchor during Season One. After his death, the Core survives by merging into the Bloody Gull.

## Symbiosis

- amplifies Compass signal
- grounds the spyglass
- sharpens Shell clarity
- aligns with `Memory of Kings`
- depends on Jeb as storm-anchor and Sophia as resonance pressure

## Faction Pressure

Most outsiders would misread it as a power source. In truth it is a survival-harmony relic, and its greatest value is keeping the system from tearing itself apart.

## Episode Status

Active from Episode 101. Cracks and transfers during the Red death arc, then lives in the Gull by Episode 109.

## Failure Test

Flag drift if the Core behaves like a battery, ignores crew harmony, or survives Red's arc without structural consequence.
