# Compass of Ley Currents

## Core Identity

The Compass of Ley Currents is a bloodline-bound navigation relic that points toward older truth, not ordinary geography. It remembers routes the world has already lost.

## Function

- charts ancient ley currents and fracture routes
- guides movement through liminal and unstable zones
- detects temporal drift and buried passage pressure
- aligns to memory-patterns rather than present-day certainty

## Limitation And Cost

- shows what was, not always what is
- scorches Sophia through bloodline burn and ley feedback
- erodes present identity through memory intrusion
- leaves permanent tidal or ley-mark scarring with overuse

## Bearer Rule

Sophia Briggs is the canon-locked bearer. The relic responds to Atlantean bloodline resonance and should not behave like a neutral tool.

## Symbiosis

- works in pair with `Shell of Memory`
- gains visual confirmation through `Spyglass of Storms`
- grows heavier and more dangerous near `Memory of Kings`

## Faction Pressure

The Compass is sacred to legacy navigation systems, envied by Phoenician imitators, and treated as a convergence trigger by factions who understand the larger circuit.

## Episode Status

Season One active relic. It is one of the main pressure points in Sophia's awakening arc.

## Failure Test

Flag drift if the Compass behaves like GPS, opens routes without cost, or works cleanly for non-bloodline users.
