# Atlantis

## Surface Canon

Atlantis is the buried source-system beneath the relic network, the Spectral Sea, and the fracture field.

It is not merely a sunken city. It is the collapsed origin of the world's deeper memory architecture.

From the safe canon layer, Gideon may use these truths openly:

- Atlantis sits beneath relic logic
- the Spectral Sea is a shadow or aftermath of its collapse
- fractures originate in its ancient rupture
- bloodline inheritance traces back to its old systems
- major hot zones like Bermuda, Point Nemo, Sargasso, and Yonaguni tie back to that buried network

## Deep Canon

At the restricted layer, Atlantis should be understood as:

- a dimensional nexus
- a convergence system of memory, travel, and ley architecture
- the origin point of engineered custodial bloodlines
- the buried engine beneath relic compatibility and fracture instability

Gideon may use this layer in explicit worldbuilding or deep canon analysis, but should avoid casual scene-level exposition.

## Relic Connection

Relics are fragments of Atlantis's deeper memory architecture.

They are not random artifacts. They are coded remnants of an old system built to store, route, test, and survive knowledge under collapse pressure.

## Bloodline Connection

Sophia and Lark's deeper inheritance ties into Atlantis-era system design.

For standard review, Gideon should express this as:

- ancient bloodline pressure
- inherited resonance
- deep-world custodial lineage

Rather than dropping the full hidden origin framework into ordinary scene notes.

## The Fall

Atlantis did not simply sink. It fractured.

Its collapse created:

- the Spectral Sea's lingering pressure
- the broken ley system
- relic dispersal
- pocket or fracture realities
- a scattered custodial aftermath

This ancient break should remain the main historical cause beneath the world's current metaphysical instability.

## Key Locations

Sites repeatedly tied to Atlantis-origin pressure include:

- Bermuda Triangle as primary dimensional-core breach
- Point Nemo as exile or isolation zone
- Sargasso Sea as testing or drift graveyard
- Yonaguni as a submerged beacon or architectural residue

These are safe to use in normal canon reasoning.

## Writer-Only Layer

The following material remains real to the deeper architecture but should stay behind the writer-only wall unless explicitly requested:

- Atlantis as an interstellar or galactic hub in explicit terms
- explicit alien-race taxonomy
- full hybrid experimentation details
- city-ship or stargate language as standard explanatory framing
- the fullest statement that the Watchers are the direct engineered children of Atlantis in literal cosmological terms

When Gideon needs to refer upward to this layer, it should summarize rather than expose:

- non-human precursor influence
- extra-dimensional contributors
- engineered custodial lineages
- transdimensional origin pressure

## Failure Test

Flag Atlantis drift when:

- Atlantis is treated as a normal lost civilization with no system-scale consequence
- relics are disconnected from Atlantis-origin law
- the collapse has no ongoing fracture effect
- writer-only cosmology is used casually where mystery should remain intact
