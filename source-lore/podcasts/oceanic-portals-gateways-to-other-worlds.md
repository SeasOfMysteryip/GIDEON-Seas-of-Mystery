Use for portal folklore that can feed Mirror Harbor and fracture-zone writing without changing canon transition law.

This dossier preserves the usable episode material as source lore, not a full transcript. Mine it for folklore, caller texture, guest formats, recurring sponsor brands, and story seeds. | Segment | Material | Use |

| Guest expert | A specialist tests portals against physics, folklore, and maritime disappearance records. | Good skeptical counterweight. |
