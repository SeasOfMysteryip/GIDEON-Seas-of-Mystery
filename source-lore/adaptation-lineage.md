# Adaptation Lineage

`05B_LORE` is source material, not just appendix material.

It appears to preserve the earlier narrative strata that helped shape `Seas of Mystery` from dramatized radio-show form into the current 10-episode story arc and larger world build.

## Primary Functions

- preserve podcast-era voice and world-entry framing
- preserve song and chant material that informs Old Red and relic-harmonic language
- preserve Tales From the Deck material that later feeds characters, ship stories, scenarios, and Bloody Gull oral culture

## Repo Rule

Use `source-lore/` as:

- origin memory
- tonal reference
- adaptation reference
- cultural material that may inform canon

Do not treat all source-lore claims as locked truth automatically.

Promote specific elements into `canon/` only when they have been explicitly adopted into:

- locked canon files
- imported episode scripts
- continuity notes
- character, relic, faction, fleet, or deep-myth doctrine
