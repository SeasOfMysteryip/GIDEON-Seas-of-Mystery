# In-World Brands

## Use Rule

These brands come from podcast commercial breaks and are preserved as shared-universe texture. Treat them like recurring background products, taverns, services, stores, ads, props, and local color. They can appear on signs, radio breaks, receipts, tourist shelves, dock posters, tavern menus, ship supplies, and joke merchandise.

They are not canon plot law by default. Promote a brand to canon only when a scene, episode card, or canon file gives it story function.

## Commercial Logic

- Ads can interrupt heavy lore with ordinary-world texture.
- Repeated brands make the podcast ecosystem feel like a real shared media space.
- Some brands can stay jokes; others can become fronts, meeting points, props, or faction-adjacent businesses later.
- Keep ad copy short when used on the page. The joke is stronger when the brand feels real and familiar.
