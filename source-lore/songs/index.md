# Songs Index

This folder preserves songs, chants, dirges, drinking songs, battle calls, and harmonic fragments from the source-lore layer.

## Use Rule

Songs are page-usable source material. Keep the full lyrics available, but do not assume every lyric is literal canon law. Songs can be warning, myth, exaggeration, grief, ritual, tavern performance, relic-adjacent language, or crew culture.

## Preserve

- recurring refrains
- performance context
- who sings the song
- whether the song behaves as warning, stabilization, ritual, memory trigger, tavern culture, or crew identity
