# The Lighthouse Keeper's Lament

## Use Rule

Use this as source-lore song material. The full lyric can be quoted or performed on the page when it fits the scene, but the lyrics are not literal canon law unless a canon file, episode card, or approved scene promotes a specific claim.

## Song Function

lighthouse dread, lost keepers, warning before a threshold.

## Performance Context

- Performed by: Old Red / Bloody Gull oral tradition
- Type: lament / warning song
- Best use: scene texture, tavern performance, shipboard ritual, memory trigger, or emotional refrain
- Response language: call-and-response, crew response, audience response, or band response should be treated as performance behavior, not as old source-system terminology

## Lyrics

_[Intro Soundbed: Low ley pulse hum, distant wind howl, gull cry single response]_
_Optional build: Ship timbers creak beneath vocals, ambient relic pulse faint beneath._

### Verse 1  -  Whispered, solemn, marrow-deep
On a lonely rock where the fractures cry,
Three keepers stood 'neath a storm-torn sky.
Their lamp burned bright with Watcher flame,
But the sea spares none - no blood, no name.
_Instrumental Hook: Bone Dice slow roll, distant Spyglass pulse_

### Chorus  -  Harmonized, crew-strong, a call across the void
Oh, where have ye gone, beyond the tide's pull?
Lost to the deep, where the dead men mull.
No graves, no stone, just the Spectral call,
And the lighthouse stands... with no one at all.
_response Layer: Whispering winds, ley pulse rising_

### Verse 2  -  Intensity grows, boots stomp deck
The last log writ, the storm's song spent,
But the dawn arose, and no soul went.
A meal turned cold, the clock fell still,
Yet the sea kept chanting beneath the hill.
_Instrumental Hook: Watcher whisper, stormglass crackle_

### Chorus  -  Reprise, defiant, crew voices clash with storm
Oh, where have ye gone, beyond the tide's pull?
Lost to the deep, where the dead men mull.
No graves, no stone, just the Spectral call,
And the lighthouse stands... with no one at all.
_Crew chant overlay: "Stand the light!" / "Hold the line!"_

### Outro  -  Haunting fade
_Wind swells softly, seagull cry distant. Bone Dice roll once more. Gull pulse fades to stillness._
Captain Jeb (low, thoughtful):
_"Lights burn out, but their resonances don't fade, lads. Not 'round here."_
_Full Stop._
