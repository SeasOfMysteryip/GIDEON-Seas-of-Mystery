# The Curse of Black Sand Cove

## Use Rule

Use this as source-lore song material. The full lyric can be quoted or performed on the page when it fits the scene, but the lyrics are not literal canon law unless a canon file, episode card, or approved scene promotes a specific claim.

## Song Function

haunted cove, cursed gold, warning before greed.

## Performance Context

- Performed by: Bloody Gull oral tradition
- Type: curse ballad
- Best use: scene texture, tavern performance, shipboard ritual, memory trigger, or emotional refrain
- Response language: call-and-response, crew response, audience response, or band response should be treated as performance behavior, not as old source-system terminology

## Lyrics

_[Intro Soundbed: Bone Dice tumble, Shell of Memory pulse faint under rising wraith moan, Watcher whisper threading beneath stormglass hum.]_

### Verse 1  -  Scene set, marrow-deep
Gather close, me hearties, for a tale to chill the bone,
Of cursed Black Sand Cove, where greed cuts to the stone.
The moon hung cold, the stars held tight, the night was black as flame,
When we sailed the Gull into the breach, bound for the Watcher's claim.
_Pulse rise under "Watcher's claim..." response_

### Chorus  -  Crew warning chant, pulse synced
Oh, Black Sand Cove, where shadows feed,
And the wind screams lost men's creed.
Where gold gleams bright, but false it lies,
Beware the curse that never dies!
Oh-ho, oh-ho, the Keeper calls your name,
No coin can buy the soul you lose,
In Black Sand's deadly game!
_Crew response overlay: "Deadly game..." Watcher whisper trails_

### Verse 2  -  Myth depth, cursed gold revealed
The beach lay dark as Watcher's veil, the air thick with the dread,
The waves they hissed of lost men's cries, of broken oath and dead.
We found a chest of Watcher's lure, jeweled bright as day,
But the earth beneath it cracked and groaned, and death began to sway.
_Pulse quakes beneath "death began to sway..."_

### Chorus  -  Crew chant full rise
Oh, Black Sand Cove, where shadows feed,
And the wind screams lost men's creed.
Where gold gleams bright, but false it lies,
Beware the curse that never dies!
Oh-ho, oh-ho, the Keeper calls your name,
No coin can buy the soul you lose,
In Black Sand's deadly game!
_Watcher whisper: "Deadly game..." fading response_

### Bridge  -  Double-time, storm surge
A wraith rose tall, eyes burning red,
Its voice a Watcher scream.
Now drown in cursed dreams!"
We fought the storm, the specter's wrath,
The sea turned black with spite.
One by one, the crew was claimed,
Swallowed by the fight!
_Pulse accelerates, Shell of Memory hum swells_

### Verse 3  -  Escape and final warning
With but a handful left to breathe, we broke the Keeper's chain,
The cursed gold we cast away, to end the Watcher's bane.
The wraith it howled, a cry of loss, as the chest sank from the shore,
And we fled that fractured, haunted cove, to darken its myth no more.
_Pulse quiets, Watcher whisper fades to shadow_

### Instrumental Break
_Fiddle lament, Shell of Memory pulse slows, Bone Dice final rattle_

### Final Chorus  -  Slow, marrow-deep warning
Oh, Black Sand Cove, where shadows feed,
And the wind screams lost men's creed.
Where gold gleams bright, but false it lies,
Beware the curse that never dies!
Oh-ho, oh-ho, the Keeper calls your name,
No coin can buy the soul you lose,
In Black Sand's deadly game!
_Crew response: "Deadly game..." fading into resonating silence_

### Outro  -  Half-time, somber, marrow-deep
So heed me tale, ye greedy souls, and steer your course away,
For Black Sand Cove will drink your breath, and drown ye in the fray.
No gold is worth the Watcher's price, no jewel nor gleaming prize,
For in that cursed bay of death, no pirate ever survives.
Oh-ho, oh-ho, the Keeper calls your name...
Oh-ho, oh-ho, Black Sand's deadly game!
_Outro: Bone Dice tumble, Watcher whisper "Deadly game..." fades to stillness._
_[Full Stop]_
