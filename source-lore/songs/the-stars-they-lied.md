# The Stars They Lied

## Use Rule

Use this as source-lore song material. The full lyric can be quoted or performed on the page when it fits the scene, but the lyrics are not literal canon law unless a canon file, episode card, or approved scene promotes a specific claim.

## Song Function

false stars, route failure, fracture navigation, broken trust in instruments.

## Performance Context

- Performed by: Old Red / Bloody Gull oral tradition
- Type: navigation lament
- Best use: scene texture, tavern performance, shipboard ritual, memory trigger, or emotional refrain
- Response language: call-and-response, crew response, audience response, or band response should be treated as performance behavior, not as old source-system terminology

## Lyrics

_[Intro Soundbed: Spectral sea hush, Gull pulse low and weak, distant bone dice response, eerie violin drone, ship creaks, Watcher whisper threading]_

### Verse 1  -  Low, solemn, marrow-deep
Gather close, let the ley-lines swell,
A shadowed truth I've come to tell.
A night when pulse and time ran dry,
And the Sea herself forgot to cry.
_Watcher drone, soft relic hum beneath verse_

### Chorus  -  Crew harmony, storm chant
The night the stars went dark, me lads,
The ley-lines stilled, the tides went slack.
No moon to guide, no flare to see,
Just breath held tight in the fractured sea.
_response pulse on "fractured sea," Gull pulse faltering_

### Verse 2  -  Build intensity, ley response rise
Compass twisted, Spyglass blind,
The stars betrayed, no path to find.
No north, no south, no whispered guide,
Just void above, and void inside.
_Spyglass resonance fades in and out, spectral whispers under chorus_

### Chorus  -  Heavier, crew stomps sync with Gull pulse
The night the stars went dark, me lads,
The ley-lines stilled, the tides went slack.
No moon to guide, no flare to see,
Just breath held tight in the fractured sea.
_Bone Dice rattle, hull groans overlay_

### Bridge  -  Spoken-sung, atmosphere thickens
The Captain cried, "Fire the pulse!"
But relic light grew weak, convulsed.
A whisper coiled across the deck,
Cold as tides that claimed our breath.
_Low Watcher chant under "coiled across the deck," Gull heartbeat fades to near silence_

### Verse 3  -  Full spectral storm rise, chaotic soundbed
The Gull did moan, her ribs did scream,
A shadow rose from fractures deep,
A hunger no flame nor chant could keep.
_Deck rumble, stormglass cracking, resonating ship bells_

### Chorus  -  Hammered home, storm energy peaking
The night the stars went dark, me lads,
The ley-lines stilled, the tides went slack.
No moon to guide, no flare to see,
Just breath held tight in the fractured sea.
_Chorus builds with Crew battle chant overlay: "Hold the line!"_

### Verse 4  -  Pullback, ghostly resonances
At dawn the sky returned to blue,
But some were lost, and none knew who.
No trace, no mark, no shadow found,
Just names now whispers in the sound.
_Ghost fleet audio response beneath final lines_

### Final Chorus  -  Haunting, fading into the Spectral Sea
The night the stars went dark, me lads,
The sea was still, the breath ran thin.
And those who sailed that spectral void,
Were never seen... again.
_Outro: Watcher whisper, Gull pulse barely audible, soft "never seen again" fades into silence._
_[Full Stop]_
