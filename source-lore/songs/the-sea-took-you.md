# The Sea Took You

_[Intro Soundbed: Shell of Memory pulse whisper, soft bone dice tumble, acoustic guitar waltz rhythm, low wind undercurrent]_

### Verse 1  -  Low and solemn, marrow-deep
The wind still whispers through the fractured sails,
But it don't call your name no more...
The tide still climbs with Spectral wails,
Yet I'm still standin' on this ghost-lit shore...

### Chorus  -  Full band, ship heartbeat drums rise
Oh, the sea took you, she called you home,

No grave to tend, no stone to weep,

### Verse 2  -  Reflective, near-whisper
They say you crossed where sailors go,

Do you hear my voice callin' you down?
_Shell of Memory soundbed pulse rises under verse, relic hum woven faintly_

### Final Chorus  -  Strong, weathered voices full of longing
