# The Bloody Gull Boys

_[Intro Soundbed: Bone Dice tumble, stomp thunder, Watcher whisper hum, hull pulse rising]_

### Intro: Crew Unison Chant, pulse rising
Ohhh, the Bloody Gull sails strong and free -
A band of rogues upon the sea!
Through storm and fire, we stake our claim -
And carve our names in blood and flame!

### Verse 1  -  Crew heartbeat verse
Out on the ley where the stormwinds roar,
We sail as one, ever hungerin' for more.
The Bloody Gull's our home, our fire, our flame,
Our name strikes fear in the Watcher's game!
_Pulse response beneath: "Watcher's game..."_

We ride the ley storms, mighty and bold!
_Pulse and stomp in sync beneath chorus_

The horizon's our hearth, the stars guide our way,

The Bloody Gull Boys  -  they all know our name!
_Watcher whisper beneath: "Our name..."_

### Chorus  -  Full force, crew unity pulse
We're the Bloody Gull Boys, aye, we're a wild crew! [response: "Wild crew!"]
With maps to ley treasures and pints o' brew! [response: "Pints o' brew!"]

We're the Bloody Gull Boys, and we own this place!

Know it's a crew that burns like flame!
For treasures, legends, and storms we own,
The Bloody Gull Boys stand alone!
_Pulse response: "Stand alone!" Watcher whisper_

### Final Chorus  -  Choir harmony, full storm
We're the Bloody Gull Boys, aye, we're a wild crew! [response: "Wild crew!"]
From storm-swept seas to the skies so blue! [response: "Skies so blue!"]
With a heart full o' rum and a pocket of gold,
We ride the ley storms, mighty and bold!

### Outro  -  Slow fade, marrow-deep
Aye, we're the Bloody Gull Boys, the sea be our home,
Wherever it calls us, we're never alone.
