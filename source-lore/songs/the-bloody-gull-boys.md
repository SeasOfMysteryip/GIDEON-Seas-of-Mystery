# The Bloody Gull Boys

## Status

Title confirmed from the authoritative song list.

The performer lineup has been canonized as minor Bloody Gull crew:

- Guitar and vocals: Callum Thorne, topman of the Bloody Gull.
- Upright bass and vocals: Bram Pike, carpenter's mate of the Bloody Gull.
- Fiddle and/or accordion and vocals: Finnian Bell, sailmaker and rigger of the Bloody Gull.
- Drums and vocals: Toby Marlowe, bosun's runner of the Bloody Gull.

## Shipboard Role

The Bloody Gull Boys are recurring Bloody Gull crew members who perform as the ship's tourist-facing band during civilian cover operations.

They distract passengers and help preserve the cruise disguise while the core crew handles anomalies and the larger mission of finding a way back to the Spectral Sea.

They can cover sound in the room, but they cannot erase recordings, radio bleed, camera evidence, sensitive witnesses, or ship behavior.
