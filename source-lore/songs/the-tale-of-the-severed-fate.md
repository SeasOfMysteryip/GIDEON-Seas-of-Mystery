# The Tale of the Severed Fate

## Use Rule

Use this as source-lore song material. The full lyric can be quoted or performed on the page when it fits the scene, but the lyrics are not literal canon law unless a canon file, episode card, or approved scene promotes a specific claim.

severed destiny, curse mechanics, consequence song.

## Lyrics

_[Intro Soundbed: Hull groan under storm pressure, Watcher whisper thread, bone dice tumble into silence.]_

### Verse 1  -  Haunting, marrow-deep cadence
She sailed through mists where dead men lie,
A ghost in the ley, no stars in the sky.
Her crew chained fast by blood and name,
But the sea bows to none... and greed feeds the flame.
_Watcher response: "Greed feeds the flame..." fading whisper_

### Chorus  -  Crew response chant, pulse rising
Oh, Severed Fate, where do ye roam?
Adrift alone on a sea turned to stone.
The winds refuse, the tides betray,
Bound to drift 'til reckoning day.
_Pulse heartbeat fades beneath chorus, "reckoning day..." response_

### Instrumental Break
_Bone Dice shuffle, hull groan, spectral hum beneath_

### Verse 2  -  Lore deepen, curse alive
The captain cruel, his hunger wide,
Swore no gold he'd keep, though by greed he died.
A bargain struck in Watcher's night,
Left the Fate adrift, lost to light.
_Watcher chant pulse rises beneath "Watcher's night"_

### Chorus  -  Harmonies widen, ship pulse sync
Oh, Severed Fate, where do ye roam?
Adrift alone on a sea turned to stone.
The winds refuse, the tides betray,
Bound to drift 'til reckoning day.
_response: "Reckoning day..." layered Watcher whisper_

### Instrumental Break
_Ghost fleet whispers, stormglass crackle_

### Bridge  -  Scene-build peak
No port will take her, no harbor save,
No map will find her, no gods will brave.
Her crew still bound by oath and chain,
Souls held tight in hurricane's reign.
_Audio hook: Hull groan intensifies, "oath and chain..." Watcher response trails_

### Verse 3  -  Myth spotlight, Navigator fear hook
Some say ye see her when the sky bleeds red,
A shadow ship, crewed by the dead.
She lures the fool with glimmering gold,
But step aboard... and grow never old.
_Bone Dice rattle beneath, Spyglass hum falters_

### Final Chorus  -  Slow, mournful, response fade
Oh, Severed Fate, where do ye roam?
Adrift alone on a sea turned to stone.
The winds refuse, the tides betray,
Bound to drift 'til reckoning day.
_Watcher whisper: "Bound to drift..." fading into quiet pulse_

### Outro  -  Whispered Navigator warning
So if ye spy her, turn from the fray,
For once ye sail her... ye never sail away.
_Outro: Hull groan fades, Shell of Memory pulse thread dissolves, final Bone Dice tumble._
_[Full Stop]_
