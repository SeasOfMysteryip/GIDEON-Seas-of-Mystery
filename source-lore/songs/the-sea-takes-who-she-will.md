# The Sea Takes Who She Will

## Use Rule

Use this as source-lore song material. The full lyric can be quoted or performed on the page when it fits the scene, but the lyrics are not literal canon law unless a canon file, episode card, or approved scene promotes a specific claim.

## Song Function

the sea's appetite, loss, fate beyond command.

## Performance Context

- Performed by: Bloody Gull oral tradition
- Type: warning dirge
- Best use: scene texture, tavern performance, shipboard ritual, memory trigger, or emotional refrain
- Response language: call-and-response, crew response, audience response, or band response should be treated as performance behavior, not as old source-system terminology

_[Intro Soundbed: Shell of Memory pulse low, single fiddle mournful drawl, stomp rhythm heartbeat under Watcher whisper "She will..."]_

### Verse 1  -  Pulse setting, marrow warning
The night was calm, the stars held tight,
Not a whisper stirred the deep.
But the sea she spoke with a Watcher's breath,
"Come closer, lad, and sleep."

### Verse 2  -  Scene tension rise
We set our course for fracture isles,

"Drift into the endless sea."
_Shell pulse sync under "endless sea..."_
