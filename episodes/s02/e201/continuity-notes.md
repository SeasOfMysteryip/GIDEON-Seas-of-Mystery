# Episode 201 Continuity Notes

## Episode Function

Episode 201 opens Season Two by resolving the `EP108` Abyss fall while carrying Old Red's death forward instead of softening it. The episode establishes the Bloody Gull's changed state, temporarily aligns Jeb and Blackbeard against Vernon, moves through Ocracoke and Vane's near-reveal, then ends with Blackbeard betraying Jeb and taking Sophia.

## Major Emotional Turns

- The crew begins trapped in the Abyss and grieving Old Red.
- Old Red's death remains a wound, not a solved mechanism.
- Lark and Sophia become active participants in the escape through harmonization.
- Jeb accepts a temporary alliance with Blackbeard under pressure.
- Jeb's fight with Vane nearly exposes the truth of Lysara's death.
- Blackbeard kills Vane before the reveal can complete.
- Blackbeard turns on Jeb and makes Sophia's capture the Season Two opening rupture.

## Canon-Sensitive Events

- The Bloody Gull escapes the Abyss after the `EP108` fall.
- Old Red's Bone Dice, the Tuning Core, and his sacrifice become part of the Gull's changed state.
- The Vernon resolution must not erase the temporary cost of Jeb and Blackbeard's alliance.
- Ocracoke is the betrayal pressure point.
- Vane nearly reveals that Blackbeard ordered Lysara's death.
- Blackbeard kills Vane before the reveal can fully land.
- Sophia is taken by Blackbeard by episode end.

## Relic And Worldlogic Activity

- Bone Dice carry Old Red's final roll and sacrifice forward.
- Tuning Core participates in the Gull's post-Abyss state.
- The Bloody Gull is not merely repaired; it is altered by death, relic pressure, and Abyss escape.
- Sophia and Lark harmonize with relics, the Gull, and Old Red's sacrifice.
- Relic logic should align with `canon/relics/relic-circuit.md` before any canon lock.

## Character Consequence

- Jeb's grief compounds: Old Red, Lysara, and Sophia become linked wounds.
- Lark's role moves beyond explanation into active relic-circuit pressure.
- Sophia's importance to Blackbeard becomes operational, not symbolic only.
- Blackbeard confirms that alliance with Jeb is a tactic, not a thaw.
- Vane exits as an unstable witness to Blackbeard's deeper betrayal.
