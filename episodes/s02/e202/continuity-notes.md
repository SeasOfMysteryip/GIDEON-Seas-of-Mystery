# Episode 202 Continuity Notes

## Episode Function

Episode 202 converts Sophia's capture into an active captivity engine, reveals more of Blackbeard's purpose, deepens Jeb's grief and realization, expands Lark's relic-circuit knowledge through the Memory of Kings, and ends by closing Spectral Sea access from the Bloody Gull. The Gull is displaced into the Atlantic near the Bermuda Triangle, with the USS Cyclops and Benjamin Briggs reveal setting up `EP203`.

## Major Emotional Turns

- Sophia resists captivity aboard Queen Anne's Revenge through observation, argument, and small active choices.
- Blackbeard's interest in Sophia becomes clearer and more dangerous.
- Jeb grieves Old Red, Lysara, and Sophia as linked losses.
- Jeb begins to understand Blackbeard's role in the old pattern of wounds.
- Lark's research becomes urgent and consequential.
- Sophia and Lark close the Spectral Sea from opposite sides of the crisis.
- The Gull survives but is stranded in ordinary Atlantic waters.
