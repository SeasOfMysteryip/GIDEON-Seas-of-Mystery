SEAS OF MYSTERY


COLD OPEN
A1S00
INT. UNKNOWN - UNKOWN


Static cuts through the black.
Images of dead pirates glitch in and out.

The sound of the ocean glitches in time with the static.

A mechanical heartbeat thumps.

The pounding grows and slowly matches beats with the breath of an unseen storm.

A voice breaks through the waves.


JEB (V.O.)
(ragged and distorted)
        She don’t fucking let go…
        Not while there’s still blood in her tides…
        Not while there’s a cursed story left to bleed.


The video feed becomes visible but it’s raw and unstable. It cuts quick between images.

The room is covered in rusted plates with a layer of crusted salt. 
A fog curls up through the floorboards.
A compass needle rattles like it’s in a death spiral.
A woman’s eyes cut like razor blades.                    
LARK (V.O.)
            (low, frayed, but not broken)
        Why is history filled with stories about ships 
        and sailors that go missing?
            (beat)
        Or ships that come back without their crew?
        But the sea - she never forgets a soul she’s 
        Tasted.


Emergency radio bursts sound warped.
Whispers and gasps sit below a distorted child’s lullaby.

A two year old girl talks to her mother.

From below the noise, a forgotten sea shanty rises.


OLD RED (V.O., rasp)
        Star-witched and sea-bound, they dragged us low -         Maps all false and a wind that don’t blow…


The heartbeat misfires

And then the first voice fights through the static.


                    JEB (V.O.)
            (dug from the grave, half grin, thunder)
        Barrel, prayer, maybe a fuckin’ miracle…
        ‘Cause she ain’t done with me yet.


The pieces of lost footage snarl once more, each one fighting to be seen.

Then a breath. Calm.

The static clings to the edge of the frame.

Then, the footage buckles - like the film itself takes a final breath.

A title flickers between static glitch.

“Field Report - Hawthorne Expeditions: Prelim B-Roll (Recovered)”
