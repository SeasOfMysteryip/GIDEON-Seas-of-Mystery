SEAS OF MYSTERY

EPISODE 102 - THE BROKEN COMPASS









Written by:
LAND PIRATE STUDIOS


11/15/2025














REDLINE PASS








COLD OPEN

COS1 — COLD OPEN
INT. — SHIP CABIN — UNKNOWN

A monitor distorts.

A steady drip of water beats over a static hum.

Sophia’s compass ticks like a heartbeat.


JEB (V.O.)
(static whispers through monitor)
She don’t let go…


The monitor cuts to old hull plates rusting over, the face of a compass with its needle spinning, stopping, and ends on a spyglass.

In front of the monitor sits Jeb Blackwater.

A conch shell flashes in the static.


JEB (INTENSE)
(like he’s preaching to a church)
Not every man’s made for the sea.


Jeb adjusts uncomfortably.


JEB (CONT'D)
Fewer still… for these waters.
The kind that pull you through the mirror.
And hook you deep in the bone.


The image distorts and cuts to —
Jordan, pale, sweat streaking his brow.
Drew, mouth dry, terror in his eyes.
Lark’s hand, brushing across the spyglass.

The image cuts back to Jeb.

He leans in.


JEB
(climax of his sermon)
You don’t breathe her in.
You let her drown you slow.
Till you’re a part of her marrow.
And she’s a part of yours.


Jeb’s eyes flare.


JEB (CONT'D)
And the Spectral Sea?
Her?
Oh, she don’t take kind to strangers.


The footage collapses. Straight to black.


OLD RED (V.O)
Oh, the stars, they lied! They led us wrong!
The waves did hum a ghostly song,
The charts were false, the sky was fake,
And now we drift — no dawn to break…


CUT TO STATIC.




ACT ONE

A1S1 — SOPHIA
EXT. THE GULL — OPEN WATERS OFF JAPAN — DAWN

The Bloody Gull is in open water, Japan on one horizon, dawn on another.

A seabird cuts the sky.

Sophia Briggs steps from below deck, bare feet on timber planks.
She wears clothing made from salvaged ship remains.

Her compass jerks at her hip.

GUS MADDOX doesn’t look up as he checks a coil of line.

LARK watches him from the shade of the mast.


LARK
So there’s a whole other ocean.
One that eats people. Steals time.
(she looks around the deck)
And you guys are selling tickets?


GUS
(chuckles, not turning)
Call it what you want.
Ferry runs. Sightseeing for the morbidly curious.
Rich folks with more time than they have money.
(beat)
And they have a lot of fucking money.
(shifts some coil around)
Couples looking for past lives.
Myth hunters.
(turns and stares at the sunrise)
You know the type.


Lark’s lips twitch. Just a little.


LARK
Yeah. I sure do.
That’s my dad.


GUS
(nods)
Then you already get it.
Half the passengers want proof.
Some work for the people that already know it.
(beat)
Some end up here and never make it back.


Sophia walks slowly across the deck.


LARK
Is she really from 1872?
Is that really Sophia Briggs?

 
GUS
Jeb says she stepped off the Mary Celeste like it 
was yesterday.
(chuckles, brotherly)
She barely speaks now — but she didn’t speak at 
all for a long, long time.


LARK
How is that possible?



GUS
The same way the captain is a pirate from the 1700s.
He’s been here longer than she has.
              (he looks at the helm)
          The Sea don’t do time like we do, ma’am.


He watches Sophia.


GUS (CONT'D)
The compass don’t point north.
It never has.


LARK
(looking at Sophia)
Then what’s it pointing at?


The compass in Sophia’s hand tugs.

Her hand tightens.
The bow of the Bloody Gull glitches.

Before it waits’s the Dragon’s Gate.


CUT TO STATIC GLITCH.

A1S2 — THE BALLAD OF MISTRAL BAY
EXT. THE GULL — HELM — DAWN


Jeb grips the wheel, fingers drumming.


JEB
(sings the melody under his breath)
Beware the tide, beware the song,
Where the mermaids weep and wail…


STUMPY PETE snaps up.


STUMPY PETE
Cap’n, maybe, uh, ya know…
(beat)
Keep that old tune for dry land?


Jeb blinks. He stares straight ahead.


JEB
Aye. I think ye be right, Stump.


Old Red drifts along the rail, barefoot.


OLD RED (MYSTICAL)
(head tilted to the wind)
We sailed on the wind through the shadowed mist,
To the waters of Mistral Bay,
Where the legends spoke of a treasure woke,
At the break of the dawning day.


He dances along the rail.
OLD RED (CONT'D, CALM)
(soft chuckle)
Sea don’t care for silence.
She fills every tide with her song.


CUT TO WHITE.


A1S3 — IT WAITS
EXT. THE GULL — MAIN DECK — MORNING


The Bloody Gull makes its way closer to the Dragon’s Maw.

Sophia grips the rail, her jaw locked tight.

The Compass of the Drowned shudders in her other hand.
It jerks hard, the needle snapping toward her chest.

Her wrist seizes.


Sophia (LOW)
(hisses)
God—


She drops to one knee.
Her skin goes pale.

Then her veins glow and form symbols below her skin.

She bites her lip and takes measured breaths.


LARK
That doesn’t look like something you can just 
walk off, kid.


Carlos and Martin rush to catch her.

Jeb grips the helm but doesn’t look back.
His knuckles are white. His jaw is set.

The Compass spins — wild —

Then slams still.

Dead—center. Pointing at Sophia.

Her body goes still.


SOPHIA
(whispers)
We know where we have to go, Captain.


Jeb nods. Once. Slow.

Sophia stands — barely.
The Compass is still in her hand. Still aimed at her heart.

The Bloody Gull glitches through the portal.

CUT TO BLACK.

A1S4 — MIRROR HARBOR
EXT. THE GULL — DEVIL’S SEA — UNKNOWN TIME


The Bloody Gull drifts in stillness.

The water is like glass.

There is no horizon and no real sky.

The only light comes from below the water.

Sea sprites form ancient symbols beneath the Gull.

𐤂  Gimel — “Journey”


𐤈  Teth — “Hidden”

𐤈  Teth — “Serpent”


𐤃  Daleth — “Passage”

A fire crowned Mt. Fuji looms in the distance.

Gus checks lines. 

Stumpy Pete adjusts rigging, eyes locked on the symbols below the water.

At the tech station, Linda drums the table in front of her glitched tablet. Her lips are thin and her eyes are sharp.

Carlos rides the drone controls with a frown on his brow.

Sprites rise to meet the drone.

The feed from the drone jitters.

A rusty sloop drifts off the Gull’s port side.

One of its crew lifts a box tagged with symbols that glow blue. Black tape covers them.

One crew member just stares.

Farther off, a ferry boat floats its name scrubbed, replaced with “SEA CLAIMS ALL” in red spray paint.

A child peers over the side.

Something below the surface shifts.

No one acknowledges the Gull.

The horizon glitches and a storm appears.

On the quarterdeck — JEB stands at the rail.

STUMPY PETE steps up beside him, his voice low and wary.


STUMPY PETE (LOW)
(he gives Jeb an uneasy look)
Sea feels like it’s resurrecting something that’s 
best left buried, Jeb.



Jeb doesn’t turn.


JEB
Aye, mate. She remembers well.


He takes a long pull off of a fresh joint.
Holds the smoke in for an extra moment.


JEB (CONT'D)
(smoke leaves his mouth as he talks)
Phoenicians sailed these tides before our 
kind dreamt of open water.
They rode the currents of the earth, ley hub to 
ley hub, fracture zone to fracture zone.


His hand grazes the rail.


JEB (CONT'D)
(looks at the rail like it’s talking to him)
Mapped the storm long before the Watchers cast 
their first net.


Sprites swirl below into Phoenician shapes again.


STUMPY PETE
(looks down at the water below)
Their work?



JEB
(he nods)
They didn’t chart the sea.
No. They hacked it.
(pulls on his joint)
Created their own leylines and used ‘em to take 
Atlantis.


Gus approaches his captain and his best friend.


GUS
Who won?
Between Atlantis and Phoenicia? Really?

There's silence.


JEB (SOLEMN)
Nobody.
(he smiles)
Some say the Phoenicians slit the Atlanteans’ 
throat.
Some say they both drowned in the same tide.
(beat)
All I know, boys, is this…


Jeb raises his arms, embracing the entire Spectral Sea.


JEB (CONT'D)
This is what the fucking war left behind.


The ship hums.

Sprites flare brighter and a path appears in the glow beneath the keel.

STUMPY PETE
What happens if we follow, Cap?


JEB
We find what’s waiting, Stump.
Or it finds us.


Carlos is watching his drone hover off the stern of the Gull.

He doesn’t notice the monitor going to static.
𐤏  Ayin — “Watch”

𐤇  Heth — “Boundaries”

𐤌  Mem — “Chaos”

𐤔  Shin — “Consume”

It disappears quicker than it showed itself.

CUT TO STATIC.


ACT TWO



A2S1 — THE SAMURAI
EXT. OPEN SEA — THE BLOODY GULL — NIGHT


Sophia holds the Compass tight.
Leyfire moves beneath her skin — blue and slow.

She flinches.

Jeb smokes, the blunt a red eye in the dark.

He watches a ship phase through. A moment of static before it solidifies.

The Sea Burial.

Her sails shimmer, ley—gold drips from her flanks.

Gus tightens lines.
Pete lights a cigarette, never smokes it. Just watches.

The flame quivers, windless.

Carlos sees the ship in the drone feed.


CARLOS
What the fuck is that thing?
(he looks up the Sea Burial)
Who the fuck is that thing?



GUS
That’s the Sea Burial.
(beat)
If Hideyoshi is here…
(he looks to Jeb)
That toll should be square.


Jeb nods.


JEB (low)
Aye.
But be ready anyway.


OLD RED crouches low, Bone Die dancing between his fingers.
His eyes don’t leave the water.


OLD RED
The old bones are restless tonight…


He lets the die roll across the deck timbers.

They freeze. Once glows amber gold like the sea sprites hovering above the waves.
The other is as black as obsidian.


OLD RED
Aye. The Sea herself be feeling it too.


Jeb clamps the rail.



JEB
Battle stations.
(he rips the rest of the blunt)
Ye camera shits better stay out of the fucking way.


The embers burn his finger tips. He doesn’t care.

He flicks the butt into the sprites below.

The crew moves sharp around him.

Jeb’s eyes never leave the Sea Burial.

Murakami Hideyoshi steps off the headless monk figurehead and onto the deck of the Gull.

His armor is crisp. Eyes are trench—black.

Linda stiffens, fingers hovering over the tablet in her hand.
Carlos mutters curses, drone stalling above the sea beneath it.

Sophia’s breath stutters.

The Compass shifts in her hand — spins, stops.
Spins.
Stops.

Old Red picks the bone dice up off the deck.


OLD RED
She feels it.


He lets the dice roll across his palm.


OLD RED (CONT'D)
Ship feels it.
(beat)
Oh… the Sea’s got her tide wrapped around all of 
us now…


Jeb’s gaze never breaks.


JEB
Fuck me.


Murakami stops at the top of the foredeck.


MURAKAMI (CONFIDENT)
(ice cold smile)
Blackwater.


Jeb lights another blunt and chuckles.


JEB
(gestures like he’s making an introduction)
Murakami Hideyoshi. Captain of the Sea Burial.
(hits the blunt, hard)
Ye’r a Samurai bastard.
Ye know that, right?


Jeb offers the blunt to Murakami.
Murakami hesitates.


JEB (CONT'D)
Did the Old Man send ya?


Murakami takes the blunt. As he takes a rip, his eyes drift to Sophia.

The Compass in her hand jerks. Her grip tightens.


OLD RED (WHISPER)
The Sea be holding her breath…
The samurai comes,
But the debt’s been paid…


MURAKAMI
(looks at Old Red)
ndeed he did, Jebediah.
Blackbeard is calling a Council of the Drowned 
Lords.
(he scoffs)
As a Lord, he requests your presence.


Murakami exhales a perfect smoke ring and then blows a line through it.


JEB (MOCKING)
What does he want? Parlay?
(he laughs)
We both know any pirate who uses “parlay” ain’t a 
real pirate.


Jeb takes the blunt from Murakami’s hand.

JEB (CONT'D)
And you damn well know I’ve paid my fucking debts 
to the man.



MURAKAMI
(eyes back on Sophia)
Debts… like promises, Blackwater, are never fully fulfilled.


JEB (GROWL)
(his eyes shift to Sophia)
Don’t fucking think about it.


MURAKAMI (COLD)
(chuckles)
Jebediah, Jebediah…
(he goes ice cold)
She’s never had a choice.


Sea Sprites cinch closer to the Gull.

Gus hulls more rope.
Pete’s cigarette flares but never reaches his lips.


GUS
Give me a fucking smoke, Stump.


Old Red catches the dice and slams them to the planks.


OLD RED
Bones say we move, boys.


The Gull lets out a sigh.


MURAKAMI
And now, captain?
Neither do you.
JEB
Maybe not.
But I know Edward better than anyone.


Murakami steps back — coat catching in the sea’s mist.

As he boards the Sea Burial, a shape moves behind him.

Silas Merrin.
His eyes locked on the Gull.

Sophia’s Compass spins so fast that it starts to hum and shimmer a blue—green.


OLD RED
Break the fucking bones, boys!


The Gull jerks and fog glitches away from her.

Sophia stumbles, her compass silent in her hand.

Murakami, still and sure, locks eyes with Jeb one last time.


MURAKAMI
Run while you can.
I won’t chase you Jeb.
But I will come take what was promised.


The space between the ships fractures and images from the past, present and possible futures devour the exposed energy.

Old Red throws the dice a final time.

FADE TO STATIC.
A2S2 — THE SEA SPRITES OF MISTRAL BAY
EXT. THE GULL — BELOW DECK — GALLEY — NIGHT


Lamplight flickers over the gathered crew. 

Billy Joel singing Piano Man comes from the hi—fi stereo and record player in the galley of the Gull.

“Sing us a song, you’re the Piano Man…”

Old Red paces slow, barefoot, swaying to the tune.
His carved bone dice turn in his palm.

Outside the porthole, sea sprites spiral tight, their glow ebbs and flows with the rhythm of the music.

“Well, we’re all in the mood for a melody
And you got us feelin’ alright”

Jeb stands, hands braced on the table, head dipped.

He draws breath. Heavy. Settles it in his chest.


JEB
(breathes deep)
I’ve seen strange things in the water.
But that night… with the sprites…
It changed me.


Sprites drift closer outside the Gull.


JEB (CONT'D)
Changed the way I see the sea.
Hell, maybe even the world itself.


Old Red’s dice spin faster. Sprites flare brighter.
JEB (CONT'D)
They weren’t playin’ in the waves.
Oh no. It was mapwork.
(hands lay out an invisible map)
 A map I couldn’t read.


Sprites arc across the hull.


JEB (CONT'D)
We sailed on.
Uneasy. Watched.


More sprites cluster at the hull.


JEB (CONT'D)
Then past the Southern Sea…
The light came again.
Not distant this time.
Beneath us.


Old Red is still dancing to the tune. Dice flicking fast.

“Oh, la, la—la, di—di—da
La—la di—di—da da dum…”

Sprites loop tighter.


JEB
Felt it first.
Cold as the grave.
Hull hummin’ like the sea herself had woken.


Old Red hums low.
JEB (CONT'D)
Sky was black. No stars.
Only the creak of wood.
(beat)
The breath of the wind.


The crew holds still.
Silent. Watching.


JEB
(takes a long pull on the joint)
It weren’t just glow.
It was a swarm.
Lightning in the sea like a sky turned upside 
down.


Sophia’s breath flutters.

Old Red’s dice spin to a blur.


JEB (CONT'D)
I ordered silence.
Watch them, I said.
Not a sound.
(beat)
The sprites left trails, like stardust.
They were aware. Watching us.
(beat)
Testing us.


Old Red stops pacing. His dice keep spinning.
JEB
We lowered a lantern.
The brightest ones rose to meet it.
The ones that chose to be seen.


The group gathered around Jeb sit silent. Hanging on every word.

The film crew is trying to get Jeb’s story on film.

Linda looks at Carlos and Martin with a shrug and a grin.


JEB (CONT'D)
Its body flickered.
Shiftin’ between solid and somethin’ else.
Light burning into my soul.
(exhales smoke through nostrils)
Eyes.
(beat)
The little bastard had eyes.


The dice slip from Old Red’s palm.
They clatter across the galley floor.

They stop.

The sprites outside the ship flare bright then they vanish.

Jeb doesn’t blink.
JEB
In that moment…
I saw somethin’ I’ll never explain.
Not just creatures of the sea.
Beings of the water.
Born from the marrow of the ocean.
(beat)
Or maybe far beyond this earth.


Sophia hums under her breath.


SOPHIA
Sea sprites, sea sprites, glowing in the night…


Her compass hums with her.


JEB
(takes one last drag)
They moved like nothin’ I’ve ever seen.
Not fish.
Not mermaids.
(beat)
Somethin’ from another world.


Jeb extinguishes the joint in his ashtray.


JEB
Somewhere out there…
(he looks off to nothing)
The sea still hums her name.
And I keep listening —
(he looks distracted)
For a ghost I never buried.


Old Red spins the bone dice between his fingers.


OLD RED
Well spun, Cap’n.
Well spun.


He places a hand on Jeb’s shoulder.

Jeb swallows hard.

His gaze drops to the floorboards.

The lantern flames stutter.

Jeb closes his eyes.

The lanterns die.

FADE TO BLACK.

EXT. THE GULL — MAIN DECK — PRE—DAWN


Underneath the Bloody Gull, ley threads are alive with golden light.

(a soft whisper to Wendy)
Come on now, love. Don’t make me a liar.

STUMPY PETE (CONT'D)
(hand pressed on Wendy’s hull, lovingly)
There she is.


Carlos and Martin move fast behind him getting camera gear ready.

Another sonar ping.

PING PONG.

Gus seals the last hatch. Glances to the horizon, then down.

GUS (DRY)
(places hand on Pete’s soulder)
She gonna throw hands if I don’t show her some 
love first?
STUMPY PETE
(grins, laughs)
She runs hot, brother.
And she likes what she likes.
(beat, serious now)
Just don’t let her choose the current, aye?

Multiple sonar pings fire off in quick succession.

Old Red leans on the railing, bone dice twitching in hand.


OLD RED (LOW, WEATHERED)
The Sea be breathing deep, boys.
She’s not biting, no.
She be singing us a song.


The crew stills as the Bloody Gull groans.

The red light on Linda’s camera blinks.

Martin’s boom mic captures the ambient sound.
Carlos squints at the deck’s sonar monitor — multiple hits, moving fast.


CARLOS (SHOUTS)
Linda! I’m gonna mount a GoPro in the sub!


Stumpy Pete steps behind him and peers over his shoulder.
He sees it too.
STUMPY PETE
(turns to Gus, now tight)
It’s already a storm down there, man.


GUS
(strapping gear)
Good. I like it loud.


Sophia gasps. The Compass ticks with each sonar ping.

Her fingers lock around it.


OLD RED (HAUNTING)
(he speaks slowly and deep)
There’s leviathans, whispers and prophecies grim,
Fog—wreathed relics where the monsters swim.
Captain Jeb roars through the bone deep fear —
Hoist the storm… the truth is near…


Stumpy Pete tightens the last of Wendy’s bolts.


STUMPY PETE
(to Gus)
She’s ready.


GUS
(nods)
So am I.
The sonar rips out one last warning.

PING.

Then — JEB BLACKWATER steps to the helm.

Lark is with him, looking grim. Tired.
JEB
(turns to look at Lark)
This be the place, lass.
(he gestures the open sea in front of them)
Maybe you can figure out what happened.


OLD RED (DARK, HAUNTING)
(he rolls his dice on the deck)
With a “Yo ho ho” and the Spectral hum,
Beware the tides where the dead men come.
(eerie smile and glowing eyes)
From the rifts below to the fractured sky,
In the Spectral Sea — we bleed or we die.

ACT FOUR
A4S1 — THE MERMAIDS
EXT. SPECTRAL SEA — APPROACHING THE MEMORY CHAMBER — NIGHT


Jeb, Lark, Linda and Old Red row in unison while Sophia sits at the bow.

Phoenician symbols twist in the current.

𐤀 — Aleph Strength
𐤂— Gimel Journey
𐤄 — He Revelation
𐤈 — Teth Hidden
𐤉 — Yodh Power

The Spectral Sea is pushed from it.

Old Red brushes the pouch around his neck, the bone dice inside turn and rattle at his touch.

The sea it takes, the sea won’t share!
Gold so bright, but touch ye not,
Lest ye drown in Neptune’s plot!

If ye see a shimmer deep, where no sun ought to 
shine…
(he chuckles)
