# Episode 108 Continuity Notes

## Episode Function

Episode 108 (`Into the Abyss`):

- rescues Sophia from the Sea Burial through Darak's bargain
- returns Sophia changed by Compass, Chronometer, and bloodline pressure
- completes the locket trade between Jeb and Darak
- turns the Red Horizon repair pause into a renewed Vane/Murakami attack
- kills Old Red and sends the Bloody Gull into the abyss

## Finale Boundary

`EP108` is the Season One finale. It should end on Old Red's death, relic silence, fracture backlash, and the Bloody Gull falling into the Abyss. Do not resolve the escape here; the Abyss drift, relic-circuit explanation, Gull rebirth, and legacy-vessel transformation belong to the Season Two opening.
