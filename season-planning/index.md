# Season Planning Index

This folder holds season-scale planning material extracted from `00_Canon Bible Core` that does not belong in constitutional canon.

Use this area for:

- season threadwork
- future-season structure
- planning frames
- story-development scaffolding that is useful but not part of Core law

## Authority Rule

Files in this folder may be strong planning guidance, but they do not outrank:

- `canon/core/`
- `canon/deep-myth/`
- locked character, relic, faction, fleet, and tactics files

When planning material conflicts with locked canon, locked canon wins.
