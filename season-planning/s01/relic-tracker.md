# Season One Relic Tracker

Fast tracker pass built from the current Season One `script.md` files. This is a state-and-pressure map, not a full canon audit.

## Scope

Tracked active Season One relics:

- Compass of Ley Currents
- Shell of Memory
- Spyglass of Storms / Echo of the Tides
- Cutlass of Surge
- Tuning Core
- Memory of Kings
- Abyssal Chronometer
- Spectral Beacon
- Crown of the Abyss
- Blackbeard's Ring
- Lysara's Locket

## Naming Watch

| Script Term | Canon Tracker Term | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `Compass of the Drowned` | `Compass of Ley Currents` | Appears in early scripts as an active name. Canon normalization prefers `Compass of Ley Currents`. |
| `Echo of the Tides` | `Spyglass of Storms` | Valid alias pressure, but canon primary name is `Spyglass of Storms`. |
| `conch shell` | `Shell of Memory` | Early acquisition language; normalize when referring to the relic as canon object. |
